Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 8, 2026  ·  Contact: PlayPrepApp@gmail.com

Thank you for using PlayPrep. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to being transparent and keeping your information safe.

1. Who We Are

PlayPrep is an independent mobile application designed to help parents, teachers, and homeschoolers manage activities, crafts, field trips, and learning plans for children. PlayPrep is operated by Cassandra Skenandore, an individual developer based in Indiana, USA.

2. Who This App Is For

PlayPrep is intended for use by adults (age 13 and older), including parents, educators, and caregivers. The app is not directed at children. Any information about children — such as age ranges or interests — is entered by a parent or guardian on behalf of the child and is stored locally on your device.

By using PlayPrep, you confirm that you are at least 13 years old. If you are under 18, you confirm that a parent or guardian has reviewed and agreed to this policy on your behalf.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

All data you enter into PlayPrep — including activity details, supply lists, notes, photos, child profile names, age groups, and interests — is stored locally on your device. We do not currently collect or transmit this information to external servers.

Specifically, the app may store:

  • Activity titles, descriptions, steps, tips, and supply lists
  • Photos you add to activities or places (stored locally on your device)
  • Child profile information such as nicknames, age groups, and interest preferences
  • Places and field trip details you add
  • Planning and scheduling data
  • Shopping list and supply closet data

3.2 Imported Content

PlayPrep offers a URL import feature that allows you to pre-fill activity details from a web page. When you use this feature:

  • The source URL is permanently associated with the imported activity and cannot be removed, ensuring attribution to the original creator is always preserved.
  • You are responsible for ensuring your use of imported content complies with the original creator's terms of use and copyright.
  • PlayPrep does not claim ownership of any imported content. We are a tool that helps you organize content you choose to reference.

3.3 Information Collected Automatically

In the current version of PlayPrep, we do not collect analytics, usage data, or crash reports automatically. Future versions of the app may include anonymous crash reporting and analytics to help us improve the app. We will update this policy before enabling such features.

3.4 Information NOT Collected

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information (no account system is currently required)
  • Payment information (handled entirely by Apple's App Store)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Any data from children directly

4. How We Use Your Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device in the current version, we do not process your personal information on our servers. Your data is used solely to provide the features of the app — organizing activities, managing supplies, and planning.

5. Future Premium Features & Cloud Storage

Future versions of PlayPrep may offer a premium subscription that includes cloud backup and sync. If and when cloud storage is introduced:

  • We will update this Privacy Policy before enabling cloud features.
  • You will be given clear notice and the opportunity to opt in.
  • Your data will be encrypted in transit and at rest.
  • You will be able to export or delete your cloud data at any time.
  • We will disclose which cloud service provider is used to store your data.

Until cloud features are available, all your data remains on your device and is subject to your device's own backup settings (e.g., iCloud backup).

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Since all data is currently local to your device, no data transfer occurs outside of your device.

In future versions, we may use third-party services for cloud storage, crash reporting, and subscription management. When such services are introduced, we will name them specifically in an updated policy.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data lives on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear All Data" option in the app's settings menu
  • Deleting the PlayPrep app from your device, which removes all locally stored data

8. Children's Privacy

PlayPrep is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app is designed to be used by adults managing activities for children in their care.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through the app, please contact us at PlayPrepApp@gmail.com and we will take appropriate steps.

9. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your data. Since PlayPrep currently stores all data locally on your device, security depends in part on your device's security settings. When cloud features are introduced, we will implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.

10. Third-Party Links & Content

PlayPrep may display links to third-party websites (e.g., source URLs for imported activities). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

11. Your Rights (US Users)

As a US-based user, you have the right to access the data you've entered into the app, delete your data at any time, and request information about any future data practices by contacting us.

California residents: PlayPrep does not sell personal information. We do not engage in cross-context behavioral advertising. For questions about your California privacy rights, contact PlayPrepApp@gmail.com.

12. International Users

PlayPrep is operated from the United States. If you are accessing the app from outside the US, please be aware that your information may be subject to US laws. If we begin offering services to EU/EEA users, we will update this policy to include GDPR-specific provisions.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you through an app update and update the "Effective Date" at the top of this document. Continued use of PlayPrep after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
