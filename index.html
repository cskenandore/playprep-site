Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

PlayPrep is focused. It does one thing well: helps you find, save, and actually do great activities with kids.

📚

Activity Library

Save activities from anywhere — paste a URL, enter manually, or browse the built-in PlayPrep Library of 50+ curated activities for every age group.

🧺

Supply Closet

Track what craft supplies you have on hand. PlayPrep automatically shows you which activities you can do right now with what you've got.

📅

Activity Planner

Schedule activities for specific days, set up recurring events, and see your week at a glance. Your plan shows up right on the home screen.

🗺️

Places & Field Trips

Save museums, parks, nature centers, and anywhere else you love to take kids. Track which places you've visited and which are on your wish list.

🗂️

Collections

Group activities into themed collections — "Summer bucket list," "Fine motor skills," "Holiday crafts." Smart collections build themselves automatically.

🛒

Shopping List

Missing supplies for an activity? Add them to your shopping list in one tap. Track quantities, check off items in the store, and add directly to your closet.

✦

Spark AI

Describe what you're looking for and Spark suggests activities tailored to your kids' ages, your supplies, and your interests. No more blank-page moments.

▶️

Play Mode

Step-by-step guidance as you do the activity. See supply lists, instructions, tips, and step photos — all hands-free while you're in the middle of crafting.
